Qi Fang(方淇)

I am a graduate student at the Department of Computer Science, University of Toronto. I am interested in general-purpose robotics that exhibit several key capabilities:

  • Visual and behavioral robustness
  • Autonomous operation with strong generalization
  • Self-optimization over time
  • Interpretable competencies
  • Responsiveness to human emotional needs

My broader interests extend to related fields including: Reinforcement Learning (RL), language and vision models (LLMs, VLMs, VLAs), Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), and trustworthy AI systems.

I completed my Bachelor of Computer Science at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT). I collaborated with Prof. Chen Feng and Dr. Felix Juefei Xu at AI4CE LAB, New York University.

I am open to research collaborations. Feel free to reach me via qifang at cs.toronto.edu
